Hi I hope this can help you. 


Code:ABB Modbus adress      =    Real Modbus adress%MW0.0               =   40001%MW0.1000            =   41001 Please note that the ABB AC500 PM571 V1.2 can only use the address from %MW0.0 to %MW0.2047 (40001 to 42048)

You can download a free program to test your Modbus from here www.modbustools.com.
